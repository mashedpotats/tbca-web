What We're Looking For

Requirements:

  • Eagerness to dive into the world of blockchain technologies!
  • Understanding of the design and development of backend services at scale
  • Proficient in at least one OO language like Java, Python, Scala or Ruby

Bonus Points:

  • Experience developing production level C/C++ systems
  • Familiarity with automated deployment and remote configuration tools
  • Familiarity with networking and/or cryptographic systems
  • Linux Mastery
